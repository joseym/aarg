Northwind Labs - Senior Backend Engineer (Platform)

About the role

Northwind Labs builds the data platform that powers logistics for mid-market
retailers. We are looking for a senior backend engineer to help us scale the
platform team's core services and raise the bar on reliability.

What you'll do

- Design, build, and operate distributed services in Go that handle high
  request volume with tight latency targets.
- Own services end to end, including on-call, and drive down operational toil.
- Improve observability so the team can diagnose production issues quickly.
- Mentor engineers and help establish backend best practices across the team.
- Partner with product to turn ambiguous requirements into shipped features.

What we're looking for

- 5+ years building backend services in production.
- Strong Go experience; familiarity with gRPC and event-driven architectures.
- Hands-on experience operating services on Kubernetes.
- A track record of improving reliability and reducing incident load.
- Experience mentoring other engineers.

Nice to have

- Experience with Terraform and infrastructure as code.
- Exposure to streaming systems such as Kafka.
- Background in logistics, supply chain, or fintech.
